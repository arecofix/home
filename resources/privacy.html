Return Policy

Returns

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for most items. Items must be in their original condition, with tags attached, and accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase. Certain items, such as personalized or perishable goods, are not eligible for return.

Exchanges

We offer exchanges for items of equal or lesser value. If the item you wish to exchange for is of greater value, you will be required to pay the difference. Exchanges are subject to availability and must be made within the same return period as returns.

Refunds

Refunds will be issued to the original form of payment within 7-10 business days of receiving the returned item. Please note that shipping fees are non-refundable, and any applicable restocking fees will be deducted from the refund amount.

Return Process

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team at support@shopcart.com or call us at (555) 123-4567. You will need to provide your order number and the reason for the return. Our team will provide you with a return authorization number and instructions for returning the item.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately. We will arrange for a replacement or refund, and may require photographic evidence of the damage or defect.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return: - Personalized or custom-made items - Perishable goods - Items marked as final sale - Gift cards

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our return policy, please do not hesitate to contact our customer service team. We are here to assist you and ensure your satisfaction with your purchase.
